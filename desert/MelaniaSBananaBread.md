# Melania Stoeber's Banana Bread
## Ingredients
- 2-3 over ripe bananas
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup flour
- 3/4 C sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp baking soda

## Process
1. Mix everything
2. Pour in grease bread pan
3. Bake about 1h@350 F
