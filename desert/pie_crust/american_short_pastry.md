# Short pastry
## Ingredients
- butter      16   Tbsp
- flour        2¾  cup  
- salt         1   tsp
- water        0.5 cup

## Short pastry
1. Kneed flour, salt and butter until it flakes
2. Add water until dough come together

## Notes:
* 16 Tbsp of butter could be replace by 8 Tbsp of butter and 8 Tbsp of lard

## Use
* Chicken Pot Pie
