# Pâte à chaud de maman
## Ingredients
- butter      125 g
- flour       250 g
- sugar         1 Tbsp
- water         1 tsp
- salt          1 pinch

## Process
1. Melt butter in pot with water, salt, sugar
2. Add flour and salt, mix well with a spatula
3. Spread dough by hand in the pan
