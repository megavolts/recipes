# Short pastry
## Ingredients
- flour						  250    g
- butter				      125    g
- salt							1	 pinch
- egg yolk						1
- water							3	 Tbs


## Short pastry
1. Mixed flour, butter, salt, egg yolk and water
2. Refrigerate for 1 hour

## Use
* Chicken pot pie
