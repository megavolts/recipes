# Very Sophisticated Banana Bread
## Ingredients
- 1 cup sugar (maybe brown sugar, or not)
- 1 stick butter
- 2 eggs
- 3-4 bananas
- Splash milk
- 1 teasp cinnamon
- 1 teasp baking powder
- 1 teasp baking soda
- 1 teasp salt
- 2 cups flour
- Some raisins/nuts/extra stuff

## Process
1. Preheat oven @325 F
2. Mash bananas.
3. Mix everything.
4. Pour in greased loaf pan.
5. Bake ~1 h 10 min in oven at 325 F.
